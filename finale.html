Water use

footprint is a measure of humanity’s appropriation of fresh water in volumes of water consumed and/or polluted

Water footprint is the amount of water used to produce each goods "The meets proiveded by HKScan ficilities". It allows us to find the water dependances in our company's operation/supply chain as well as lowering our water waste in accordance to regilations.
We measure water footprint in cubic metre per tourne of production.

Carbon Footprint and its causeus

Carbon Footprint definition

A carbon footprint is the total greenhouse gas (GHG) emissions caused by an individual, event, organization, service, or product, expressed as carbon dioxide equivalent. Greenhouse gases, including the carbon-containing gases carbon dioxide and methane, can be emitted through the burning of fossil fuels, land clearance and the production and consumption of food, manufactured goods, materials, wood, roads, buildings, transportation and other services.[
